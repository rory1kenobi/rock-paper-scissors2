Here's how to play...

This is a two player game.

Player 1 selects between "Rock", "Paper" or "Scissor"...

Then Player 2 selects between the same options as above.

Who wins?

Rock smashes Scissors, so Rock wins

Paper wraps Rock, so Paper wins

Scissors cut paper, so Scissors win

And if you both match, then it's a draw and you play again!
